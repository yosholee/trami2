# Aggressive SEO Strategy: Cuba Shipping From Sarasota, Bradenton, and Tampa

## Primary Objective

Make TramiXpress the dominant organic and local-search result for people looking to send packages, food, medicine, and essentials to Cuba from Sarasota, Bradenton, Tampa, and nearby Gulf Coast Florida communities.

Primary conversion: WhatsApp inquiry or booked shipment.

Secondary conversions: package tracking, quote request, phone call, Google Business Profile interaction, Facebook message.

## Positioning

TramiXpress should own this message everywhere:

> Envíos a Cuba desde Sarasota, Bradenton y Tampa. Paquetes, comida, medicinas y cajas puerta a puerta con rastreo.

English variant:

> Shipping to Cuba from Sarasota, Bradenton, and Tampa. Packages, food, medicine, and door-to-door boxes with tracking.

The strategy should focus on Spanish-first search demand, with English pages supporting broader local and family-search traffic.

### Page Template

Each page should include:

- H1 matching the city or service keyword.
- One clear WhatsApp CTA above the fold.
- Local proof: pickup/drop-off area, Bradenton base, nearby neighborhoods served.
- Services: packages, food, medicine, air, sea, door-to-door.
- Timelines and disclaimers.
- FAQ section unique to that city or service.
- Internal links to the other city and service pages.
- LocalBusiness, Service, BreadcrumbList, and FAQPage schema where relevant.

Avoid copy-pasting the same page with only city names swapped. Each city page needs local details, landmarks, neighborhoods, pickup process, and specific search intent.

## Google Business Profile

Google local visibility depends heavily on relevance, distance, and prominence. The Bradenton profile should be treated as a conversion page, not a directory listing.

Actions:

- Use the exact business name consistently: TramiXpress.
- Primary category should match the business as closely as Google allows, likely shipping service, courier service, freight forwarding service, logistics service, or similar.
- Add services with keyword-rich names: Envíos a Cuba, Paquetes a Cuba, Envío de comida a Cuba, Envío de medicinas a Cuba, Envío aéreo a Cuba, Envío marítimo a Cuba.
- Add service areas: Bradenton, Sarasota, Tampa, Lakewood Ranch, Palmetto, St Petersburg, Clearwater, Brandon, Riverview.
- Add photos weekly: boxes, office/storefront, packing, team, drop-off/pickup, branded vehicle/signage if available.
- Post weekly in Spanish and English about cutoff dates, Cuba routes, food/medicine shipments, and holiday shipping deadlines.
- Add UTM tracking to the website and appointment/WhatsApp links.
- Ask every satisfied customer for a Google review using a direct review link.

Review prompt:

> Gracias por enviar con TramiXpress. Si le fue bien con su envío a Cuba, nos ayuda muchísimo dejando una reseña aquí: [review link]. Puede mencionar desde qué ciudad envió, por ejemplo Sarasota, Bradenton o Tampa.

Do not offer incentives for reviews.

## Technical SEO

Current strengths:

- Bilingual routing exists.
- Metadata exists.
- Sitemap exists.
- Robots file exists.
- JSON-LD exists.
- Homepage already targets Sarasota, Bradenton, Tampa, and Cuba.

Technical improvements:

- Add city and service pages to `sitemap.ts`.
- Add `x-default` hreflang.
- Add `BreadcrumbList` schema for all new pages.
- Change the organization schema to a more specific `LocalBusiness` or logistics-related type where appropriate.
- Add phone number, postal code, geo coordinates, opening hours, sameAs links, and WhatsApp contact point to schema.
- Add page-specific metadata instead of relying only on locale layout metadata.
- Add optimized Open Graph images for Spanish and English.
- Add image alt text that includes real service context, not generic delivery wording.
- Use internal links from homepage sections to the new landing pages.
- Confirm Search Console, Bing Webmaster Tools, and GA4 are installed.
